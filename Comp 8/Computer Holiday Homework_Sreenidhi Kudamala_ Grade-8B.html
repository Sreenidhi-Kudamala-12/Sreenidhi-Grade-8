Top 5 Places to Visit in Ladakh




Click on the images to know more

1. Leh Palace

Built in the 17th century, Leh Palace resembles the Potala Palace of Tibet and offers panoramic views of Leh town.



2. Pangong Lake

This high-altitude lake stretches from India to China and is famous for its changing colors and serene beauty.



3. Hemis Monastery

One of the largest and richest monasteries in Ladakh, Hemis is known for its annual festival and ancient relics.



4. Nubra Valley

Located north of Leh, Nubra Valley is known for its sand dunes, Bactrian camels, and Diskit Monastery.



5. Lamayuru Monastery

Often called the “Moonland of Ladakh,” Lamayuru is one of the oldest monasteries and offers stunning lunar-like landscapes.
